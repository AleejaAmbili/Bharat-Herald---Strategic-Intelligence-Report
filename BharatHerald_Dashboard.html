Strategic Roadmap — 3-Phase Recovery Plan
Phase 1
STABILIZE
0–6 Months
  • Cut print overprinting in high-return cities (Ahmedabad 6.4%, Delhi 6.3%)
  • Renegotiate advertiser contracts — prioritise FMCG & Government categories
  • Freeze digital pilot spend until UX is fully rebuilt from ground up
  • Emergency audit: identify 2–3 editions for consolidation or downscaling
Phase 2
REBUILD
6–18 Months
  • Relaunch mobile-first WhatsApp content in Varanasi, Kanpur & Lucknow (>75% smartphone)
  • Build regional content hubs with vernacular-first editorial strategy
  • Launch digital ad packages for Real Estate & Automobile categories
  • Deploy advertiser performance dashboards to rebuild confidence
Phase 3
GROW
18–36 Months
  • Full digital-first strategy for all Tier 2 cities crossing the 70% smartphone threshold
  • Subscription model pilot in Tier 1 cities — Mumbai, Delhi, Ahmedabad
  • Cross-state programmatic ad network leveraging 10-city reach at scale
  • Data monetisation: readership analytics as premium ad intelligence product
